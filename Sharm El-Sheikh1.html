Naama Bay

Naama Bay is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Sharm El-Sheikh, known for its soft sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and stunning coral reefs. It is the vibrant heart of the city, featuring restaurants, cafes, shops, and a scenic promenade. Visitors can enjoy activities like diving, snorkeling, and glass-bottom boat tours. It is a perfect destination for relaxation and marine adventures.

Saint Catherine's Monastery

Saint Catherine's Monastery is one of the oldest monasteries in the world, located at the foot of Mount Moses in Sinai. It was founded in the 6th century AD by Emperor Justinian I and is a significant religious and historical site. The monastery houses an ancient church, a library with rare manuscripts, and the sacred Burning Bush, believed to be where Prophet Moses saw the divine fire. With its unique architecture, it attracts pilgrims and tourists from around the world.

Tiran and Sanafir

Tiran and Sanafir Islands are located at the entrance of the Gulf of Aqaba in the Red Sea. They are known for their crystal-clear waters, stunning coral reefs, and rich marine life. The islands are a perfect destination** for diving, snorkeling, and boat trips, offering a chance to see rare fish, sea turtles, and dolphins.

Soho Square

Soho Square is one of the most famous entertainment hubs in Sharm El-Sheikh, known for its vibrant and modern atmosphere. It features a variety of international restaurants, cafes, shopping stores, and dancing fountains, along with live music performances, an ice skating rink, and children's play areas. It is the perfect destination for shopping, entertainment, and nightlife, making it a favorite spot for tourists and families.

Desert safari trips

Desert safari trips in Sharm El-Sheikh offer an exciting adventure to explore sand dunes and mountains. Activities include quad biking, camel riding, watching the sunset, visiting Bedouin camps, and enjoying traditional shows and dinner. A perfect experience for adventure and nature lovers

Old Market Sharm

Old Market Sharm is a popular destination in Sharm El-Sheikh, reflecting authentic Egyptian culture. It features shops selling souvenirs, handicrafts, spices, and traditional clothing, along with local restaurants and the beautiful Al-Sahaba Mosque. A perfect place for shopping and cultural experiences!
