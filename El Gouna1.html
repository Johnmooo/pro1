Abu Tig Marina

Abu Tig Marina is one of the most iconic landmarks in El Gouna, featuring a luxurious harbor filled with yachts, upscale restaurants, and stylish cafés. Designed in a Mediterranean-inspired style, it offers a charming and vibrant atmosphere. The marina is a hub for nightlife and water activities, where visitors can enjoy sailing, diving, and water sports, making it a top destination in El Gouna.

Zeytouna Beach

Zeytouna Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in El Gouna, known for its crystal-clear waters and soft golden sands. It is located on a private island and is connected to the mainland by a charming wooden bridge. The beach offers various activities such as swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and water skiing, making it a perfect destination for relaxation and enjoying the stunning natural scenery.

El Gouna Golf Course

El Gouna Golf Course is one of the finest golf courses in Egypt, designed by world-renowned experts. It features 18 holes set amidst stunning landscapes with lush greenery, lagoons, and views of the Red Sea. The course caters to players of all skill levels, offering a unique golfing experience in a breathtaking setting, making it a top destination for golf enthusiasts worldwide.

El Gouna Museum

El Gouna Museum is a small yet fascinating museum located in El Gouna, showcasing a collection of over 90 artifacts that reflect Egypt’s rich history and culture. The exhibits include statues, manuscripts, and relics from different eras, ranging from Pharaonic to Islamic periods. With its simple yet elegant design, the museum offers visitors a unique cultural experience, making it a must-visit for history and art enthusiasts.
