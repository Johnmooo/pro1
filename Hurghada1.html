Giftun Island

Giftun Island is the first natural reserve in the Red Sea and one of the top attractions in Hurghada. It is famous for its white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs, making it a perfect destination for snorkeling, swimming, and boat trips. Visitors can enjoy boat tours, dolphin watching, and relaxing on the stunning beaches, making it an ideal spot for nature and adventure lovers.

El Gouna

El Gouna is a luxury resort town located north of Hurghada, known for its beautiful lagoons, stunning beaches, and upscale hotels. It offers various activities like snorkeling, water sports, and golf, making it a perfect destination for relaxation and outdoor adventures.

The Hurghada Aquarium Museum

The Hurghada Aquarium Museum is a great destination to explore the fascinating marine life of the Red Sea. It features rare fish species, coral reefs, and sea turtles, making it a perfect place for marine life enthusiasts to learn and enjoy.

the Sand Museum in Hurghada

The Sand Museum in Hurghada is a unique attraction featuring massive sand sculptures of historical, mythical, and military figures, crafted by international artists. The museum showcases statues of pharaohs, historical heroes, and famous movie characters, making it a great destination for art and history enthusiasts.

Makadi Water World

Makadi Water World** is the largest water park in Hurghada, featuring dozens of water slides, swimming pools, and exciting attractions, making it a perfect destination for families and adventure lovers.

Hurghada Marina

Hurghada Marina is a luxurious waterfront promenade overlooking the Red Sea and one of the city's top entertainment spots. It features high-end restaurants, cafes, shops, and vibrant nightlife, with stunning views of yachts and turquoise waters. It's the perfect place for a relaxing stroll, dining, and enjoying the seaside atmosphere.
