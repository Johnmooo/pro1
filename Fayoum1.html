Ain Al-Selein

Ain Al-Selein is one of the most famous natural springs in Fayoum, known for its fresh flowing water** surrounded by beautiful landscapes. It is a popular tourist destination, offering a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation and picnics. The area also features gardens and restaurants, making it an ideal spot for families and nature lovers.

Wadi El Rayan

Wadi El Rayan is a natural reserve located in Fayoum Governorate, famous for its stunning waterfalls, the only ones in Egypt. The area features two connected lakes surrounded by sand dunes and serves as a habitat for diverse wildlife, including gazelles, foxes, and rare birds. It is a popular destination for activities such as swimming, camping, boat rides, and sandboarding, making it one of Egypt’s most beautiful natural attractions.

Tunis Village

Tunis Village is a charming tourist village located in Fayoum, renowned for its handmade pottery craftsmanship, which attracts visitors from all over. The village offers breathtaking natural views overlooking Lake Qarun and provides a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation. It is also home to art workshops and pottery schools, making it a unique destination for art and culture enthusiasts.

Qasr Qarun

Qasr Qarun is an ancient temple located near Lake Qarun in Fayoum, dating back to the Ptolemaic era. It is known for its unique architectural design and is believed to have been dedicated to the worship of Sobek and Isis. A fascinating phenomenon occurs during the winter solstice, when the sun aligns perfectly with the temple’s inner sanctuary, showcasing the advanced astronomical knowledge of ancient Egypt.

Lake Qarun

Lake Qarun is one of the oldest and largest natural lakes in Egypt, located in Fayoum Governorate. It is known for its rich biodiversity and serves as a habitat for various migratory birds and fish species. The lake offers activities such as fishing, sailing, and dining at waterfront restaurants. Surrounded by archaeological sites, it is a unique destination that combines nature and history.

Jabal Al-Mudawara

Jabal Al-Mudawara is a natural landmark located in Wadi El Rayan, Fayoum, known for its unique circular shape, resembling a dome. It offers a stunning panoramic view of the surrounding lakes and desert, making it a perfect destination for camping, photography, and hiking. The area is especially breathtaking during sunrise and sunset, attracting nature and adventure enthusiasts.
